The American Ambassadors visit to Faraba
The visit of the US Embassy delegation, led by Mrs. Cromer, to the University of the Gambia's School of Business and Public Administration (SBPA) Faraba Bantang campus on December 6th, 2023, marked a significant event in the university's calendar. The presence of a handful of delegates from the embassy, along with the esteemed HE Kemo Conteh, President of the Students Union, Faculty Staff, and ECOMANSA added prestige to the occasion. HE Kemo Conteh's inspiring words and the importance of the occasion set the tone for the event, emphasizing the value of collaboration and exchange between institutions and international partners. His presence underscored the significance of the visit and the potential impact it could have on the university community.

GREEN LEGACY: ECOMANSA'S SUCCESSFUL TREE PLANTING EVENT AT SBPA FARABA CAMPUS
Following the successful tree planting exercise held on Saturday, November 25, 2023, at the SBPA Campus in Faraba, Ecomansa expressed heartfelt gratitude to Standard Chartered Bank, the exercise's primary sponsor, the University of the Gambia management, the Students Union, and all students who participated. This exercise, the first of its kind at Faraba Banta Campus, was aimed at giving life to the campus, as the theme depicted it: plant a tree and save the planet.
