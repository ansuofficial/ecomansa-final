Success stories

On Thursday, February 15th, 2024, at 11 am, a fresh beginning unfolded as new faces joined the SBPA community. A grand event took place in the auditorium at the faculty of law to welcome our new members. The event began with lecturers conducting the first session, a tradition aimed at orienting the freshmen seated before them. As they took turns imparting words of wisdom, Dr. Lamin B Jammeh, the unit head of economics, moderated the session. Although the overseeing dean, Dr. Lamin B Ceesay, was absent, Dr. Christopher Belford extended his congratulations and encouragement to the new students on Dr. Ceesay's behalf. Afterward, Dr. Belford delivered his own message. Subsequently, all present lecturers had the opportunity to engage the freshmen with discussions about the exciting programs they oversaw, using charming melodies to capture their interest.

Ecomansa session. The moderator, Sainey Kora – the Information and Communication Minister of Ecomansa – took the microphone and introduced the 20th executive council members seated on the podium. Following this, the first speaker, Secretary-General Muhammed Kah, was invited to deliver a speech on behalf of Ecomansa. In his address, he warmly welcomed the new students to their new home away from home, emphasizing inclusivity and encouraging them to double their efforts in this new academic setting. He offered three pieces of advice: to seek out a mentor, participate in extracurricular activities, and strive for academic excellence. Subsequently, Finance Minister Lamin Jah took the stage to welcome the audience and clarify their financial obligations to Ecomansa. Following his remarks, other ministers or deputy ministers had the opportunity to share a few words with the students.

In the spirit of acclimatizing students to the vibrant atmosphere of SBPA, the council organized a football match, where lecturers, council members, old, and new students participated in a friendly game. It was indeed a memorable day for the new intake. The new intakes having tasted the exiting and exhibiting environment poised to be their new home for the next four years, left for home after encountering a jolly excitement.
